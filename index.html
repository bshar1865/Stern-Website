About Me

I'm an iOS and UWP developer specializing in Swift, SwiftUI, and C++. I work on high-performance emulation and sideloading tools like XBSX2, DolphinUWP, SwiftScraper, and SideStore. My focus is on delivering seamless user experiences on iOS and Xbox platforms.

XBSX2

XBSX2 is a fork of PCSX2, a free and open-source PlayStation 2 (PS2) emulator. This project allows you to emulate PS2 hardware on Xbox, enabling you to play your favorite PS2 games with added features and improvements.

GitHub: XboxEmulationHub/XBSX2

DolphinUWP

DolphinUWP is a fork of Dolphin Emulator, supporting GameCube and Wii games on UWP/Xbox. It is part of an ongoing effort to bring quality emulation to a wide range of platforms.

GitHub: SternXD/dolphin

SideStore

SideStore is an iOS app that enables users to sideload apps onto their devices using just their Apple ID. It automatically refreshes apps to prevent expiration and keeps them active with your personal development certificate.

GitHub: SideStore/SideStore

SwiftScraper

SwiftScraper is an iOS app designed to scrape and analyze web content directly from your device. It extracts titles, paragraphs, links, and images from websites for easy browsing and analysis.

GitHub: SternXD/SwiftScraper
